# Retail-Sales-Analytics-Using-Excel-SQL-Power-BI

## 🔹 Project Overview

Data was taken from a structured retail sales dataset with multiple related tables (customers, orders, products, stores, etc.)

Built an end-to-end analytics pipeline using Excel, SQL, and Power BI

Focused on sales performance, customer behavior, inventory, and store analysis

Created interactive dashboards for business insights and KPI tracking

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies Used

SQL → Data extraction, joins, aggregations, and analysis

Microsoft Excel → Data cleaning and preprocessing

Power BI → Dashboard creation and data visualization

## 🔹 Key Features

Store-wise and staff-wise sales performance analysis

Customer segmentation based on purchase behavior

Product and brand-level sales & quantity insights

Inventory tracking with low-stock alerts

Geographic analysis of customer distribution and sales

## 🔹 Data Analysis Performed (SQL)

Joined multiple tables (orders, products, customers) to generate detailed sales reports

Calculated total sales per store and ranked performance

Segmented customers into low, medium, and high-value groups

Identified top-performing locations based on revenue

Detected low-stock products for inventory optimization

## 🔹 Dashboard Insights (Power BI)

### Customer Analysis

Majority customers from a single region (New York ~70%)

Very low repeat purchase rate (<10%)

Sales Trends

Sales fluctuated between $200K–$420K

Peak near $900K followed by sharp decline

### Product Analysis

High-value products generate more revenue

Low-cost products sell in higher quantities

### Brand Analysis

Trek leads in revenue

Electra leads in quantity sold

### Store & Staff Performance

One store contributes ~70% of total sales

Sales heavily dependent on top-performing staff

## 🔹 Key Business Insights

Low customer retention and poor contact data availability

Heavy dependence on single store and brand

Sales driven by one-time events instead of consistent demand

Inventory imbalance (low stock vs overstock issues)

Uneven staff performance across stores

## 🔹 Business Recommendations

Implement loyalty and referral programs to improve retention

Improve customer data collection for targeted marketing

Plan year-round marketing campaigns to stabilize revenue

Use inventory optimization techniques (ABC analysis, alerts)

Diversify sales across stores, regions, and product categories

Train and balance performance across staff

## 🔹 Project Outcome

Enabled data-driven decision-making

Identified key areas for revenue growth and cost optimization

Improved understanding of customer behavior and sales trends

Built a scalable analytics solution for business performance monitoring
